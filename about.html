About Us

Global Campaign to Demand Climate Justice (DCJ) is a network of over 200 climate and human rights organizations working at international, regional and local level on issues of climate justice and just transition.

DCJ formed in 2012 in Bangkok with the intention of being a campaign vehicle. It focused on bringing on groups and organizations together rather than unaffiliated individuals. It launched with 200 groups agreeing to a common platform.

Since then, DCJ has ben campaigning on energy transformation (through the Reclaim Power mobilizations) and food, land, and water, as well as establishing itself as the convener of climate justice groups in the United Nations Framework Convention on Climate Change, where DCJ makes up one half of the Environmental NGO Constituency alongside Climate Action Network.
