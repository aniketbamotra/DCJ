GLOBAL COORDINATING COMMITTEE

Based on the platform, directions and strategic priorities agreed by the DCJ membership, the DCJ Global Coordinating Committee (DCJ CC) develops and agrees major plans, priorities and strategic vision, and shares responsibility for collective implementation of major activities.

The DCJ Global Coordinating Committee is a vehicle to make real the cooperation between our organizations. The DCJ CC consists of volunteers from the organizational membership as well as members of the DCJ Global Secretariat.
